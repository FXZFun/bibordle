How to Play

Guess the word from the Bible in six tries

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word from the EHV.

After each guess, the tiles will change colors to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples

J E S U S

The letter U is in the correct spot

The letter E is in the word but in the wrong spot

The letter J is not in the word
